This project contains a graphics demo using GLSL.



Controls:

Use the 'R', 'G' and 'B' keys on your keyboard to toggel on or off their respected colour channels.
(note: turn on multiple colour channels simultaniusly to get a mix of colours)

Use the RIGHT, LEFT, UP & DOWN arrow keys to rotate the camera.

Use W, A, S & D TO MOVE;

use q and e for up and down;
